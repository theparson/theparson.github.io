How do I submit a letter to the editor?

Email your letter to letters@theparson.co.uk. Please include your name and contact details. Letters may be edited for clarity and length before publication.

How do I submit a local event?

We list local events in our monthly “What’s On”. Email ads@theparson.co.uk with the title, date, time, venue and a short description. Send listings a few weeks before month-start to meet print deadlines.

Updated Nov 2025
Source: site homepage/contact.

How do I publish an announcement?

For announcements such as births, deaths and engagements, email your wording to hello@theparson.co.uk and the team will advise the next available issue and format.

Updated Nov 2025

How do I contact the editor?

For editorial queries and story ideas, email the editor at hector@theparson.co.uk. For general enquiries, email hello@theparson.co.uk.
