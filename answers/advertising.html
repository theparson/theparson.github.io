How do I advertise in The Parson?

Email ads@theparson.co.uk to book your advert in the next issue. We publish monthly and print in full colour. Formats include quarter, third, half, full page and double-page spread. Prices are per issue, with discounts for repeated adverts, charitable causes and eligible local businesses in SW6. For deadlines and artwork guidance, please email the team.

Do local SW6 businesses get a discount?

Yes. Businesses and individuals in SW6 3, SW6 4 and SW6 5 receive a 25% local discount on display advertising prices. Mention your postcode when booking via ads@theparson.co.uk.

Can I choose where my advert appears?

We do not offer preferred positions. Adverts are placed throughout the paper, never adjacent to each other, and not on the front or back cover. This protects reader experience and overall performance.
