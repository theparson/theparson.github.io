Is The Parson free?

Yes. The Parson is the free, independent local newspaper for the Parsons Green area. It is supported by advertisers, so readers do not pay to access local news and features. Copies are delivered monthly across SW6 and extra copies are placed in local venues.

How often is it delivered?

We publish once a month. Copies are hand-delivered across SW6 at the start of each month, with extra copies placed in shops, cafés and salons around Parsons Green.

How many copies and where are they delivered?

We hand-deliver 10,000 copies across SW6 each month and place about 1,000 extra copies in local venues. If your venue would like copies, email hello@theparson.co.uk.

Which areas do you cover?

Our core footprint is Parsons Green and the wider SW6 area of Fulham. To confirm a specific street or add a venue to the drop list, email hello@theparson.co.uk.
