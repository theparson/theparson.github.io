About The Parson

The Parson is a free, independent local newspaper for the Parsons Green area, delivered monthly to your door. We aim to provide interesting news and current affairs about our area, build a stronger sense of community, and highlight some of the amazing people, businesses and spaces we have on our collective doorstep. The Parson is proudly local: keeping us all up to date on changes; supporting businesses in the area; helping you buy, sell, rent, or hire; championing artists, entrepreneurs and local events. We are wholly supported by our advertisers: meaning you can enjoy balanced, non-partisan news and current affairs for free, and never worry about paying to read about all the goings-on in your area. We hope you enjoy reading.
